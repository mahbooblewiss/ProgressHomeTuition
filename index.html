About Us

Progress Home Tuition is a premier educational institution in Patna, dedicated to providing quality education to students from Class 1 to 12. Under the expert guidance of Irfan Sir, we strive to create a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

Our team of experienced educators is committed to helping students achieve their full potential through personalized attention and innovative teaching methodologies.

We offer comprehensive coaching for CBSE, BSEB, and ICSE curricula, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for their academic challenges and future endeavors.
